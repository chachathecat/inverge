## Design Intent

The product should feel calm, premium, operational, and low-noise. It should not feel like a public archive, generic dashboard SaaS, pass-probability calculator, score-first grading surface, or marketing-first landing page.

The first screen of a future app experience should make the operating task visible, not explain the product in a hero-only marketing layout.
