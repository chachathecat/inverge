# Inverge Business Model

## Principle

Inverge monetization must not distort the learning loop. The product should first prove that Capture, diagnosis, review, prediction, recommendation, execution, and retry/rewrite improve learner execution for 감정평가사 1차 and 2차.

No payment-first flow should block initial learning. Do not monetize anxiety, mistakes, or basic recovery too early.

## Free

Purpose: let learners understand the operating loop.

Includes:

- limited Capture entries;
- small Today Plan;
- basic O/X and concept-card review;
- limited 2차 rewrite samples;
- ethical morning brief preview;
- data-boundary transparency.

Free should not include broad archive browsing, unlimited generation, or premium managed support.

## Pro

Purpose: individual learner execution system.

Includes:

- expanded Capture-to-OX and review queue;
- full Today Plan max 3 prioritization;
- curriculum-based weak-node tracking;
- explanation ladder across O/X, cloze, accounting template, rewrite, CASIO, and issue spotting;
- schedule tracks for 1차 and 2차;
- personal progress and risk signals.

## Pass OS

Purpose: high-commitment exam operating system.

Includes:

- advanced schedule adaptation;
- deeper 2차 rewrite history;
- CASIO calculation traceability support;
- 과락 risk monitoring;
- final-month compression planning;
- richer reference-data grounding after official verification.

Pass OS should still avoid AI final-judgment claims and score-only endpoints.

## Cohort / managed premium

Purpose: structured operations for learners who want managed accountability.

Includes:

- cohort pacing;
- operator-reviewed milestones;
- managed weak-node reviews;
- periodic planning support;
- optional instructor/academy coordination only when clearly separated from learner UI and never framed as AI final grading.

This tier must preserve consent, tenant separation, and clear boundaries if connected to any instructor-facing process.

## What must not be monetized too early

- Basic mistake recovery.
- Clear data-boundary controls.
- Ability to retry/rewrite after feedback.
- Essential due review.
- Learner export/delete controls.
- Safety copy and official-verification status.
- Basic explanation of why a task is recommended.

## Beta pricing hypothesis

These are hypotheses for research, not implemented prices:

- Free: discovery and trust-building.
- Pro: monthly individual plan for serious 1차/2차 learners.
- Pass OS: higher monthly or seasonal plan for exam-window execution.
- Cohort / managed premium: cohort-based price with human operations support.

Pricing must be validated only after retention, learning outcomes, and trust metrics are healthy.
